Padma Purana

The Padma Purana is one of the eighteen major Puranas in Hinduism, known for its encyclopedic nature and extensive coverage of cosmology, mythology, religious rituals, and pilgrimage guides.
The seven sections of Padma Purana are:
Sṛṣṭikhaṇḍa (Creation Section)
Bhūmikhaṇḍa (Earth Section)
Svargakhaṇḍa (Heaven Section)
Brahmakhaṇḍa (Brahma Section)
Pātālakhaṇḍa (Underworld Section)
Uttarakhaṇḍa (Final Section or Later Section)
Kriyāyogakhaṇḍa (Section on Ritual Yoga)
Each deals with various aspects of cosmology, rituals, geography, ethics, and mythology.
