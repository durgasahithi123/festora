Nrahmanda Purana

The Brahmanda Purana is one of the eighteen Mahapuranas in Hinduism, traditionally attributed to the sage Vyasa. It is an encyclopedic Sanskrit text consisting of about 12,000 to 14,000 verses. The name "Brahmanda" means "Cosmic Egg," referring to the universe's origin described in the text.
The Brahmanda Purana combines philosophical, devotional, ethical, and practical aspects of Hindu religion, making it a comprehensive guide to spirituality and life in traditional Hindu society .
