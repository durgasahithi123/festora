Kanaka Durga Devi Temple

History:
Kanaka Durga Temple, situated on the Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, is dedicated to Goddess Durga. The temple has a history that dates back to several centuries and is famous for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance.
Puja Rituals:
Devotees partake in daily puja including abhishekam, archana, and aalankaram. The temple conducts special rituals during Navratri and other auspicious days.
Festivals Celebrated:
Navratri is the major festival celebrated with grandeur, attracting thousands of devotees. Other festivals include Vijayadashami and other regional celebrations honoring the Goddess.
