Lotus Temple

History & Architecture:
The Lotus Temple, also known as the Bahá'í House of Worship, is located in Delhi. Completed in 1986, it is notable for its unique lotus-shaped architecture symbolizing purity and peace. The temple welcomes people of all faiths for meditation and prayer in a serene environment.
Visiting Information:
Lotus Temple is open daily except Mondays and attracts millions of visitors annually. It offers tranquil gardens and a quiet atmosphere for reflection, with no sermons or rituals for specific religions.
