Mahapurana

The Mahapurana is a major Jain text composed by Acharya Jinasena and Gunabhadra. It narrates the history of Jain Tirthankaras, legends, cosmology, and doctrines, serving as a rich source of Jain mythology and religious tradition.

This extensive scripture consists of two parts, Adipurana and Uttarapurana, describing the lives of spiritual heroes and teachings.
