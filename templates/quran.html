Quran

The Quran is the holy scripture of Islam, believed to be the literal word of God (Allah) revealed to Prophet Muhammad over 23 years. It comprises 114 chapters (surahs) and guides spiritual, ethical, social, and legal aspects of Muslim life.

It is recited and memorized worldwide and serves as the foundation of Islamic theology and practice.
