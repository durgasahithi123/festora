Meenakshi Temple

History:
The Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is an ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (Parvati) and Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva). The temple's historic origins date back over 2,500 years, magnificently blending Dravidian architecture with carven towers and pillared halls.
Puja Rituals:
Elaborate daily pujas, including abhishekam, alangaram, neivethanam, and deepa aradanai, are performed. The temple follows a strict schedule of sevas with vibrant rituals and music enhancing devotees' spiritual experiences.
Festivals Celebrated:
The annual Meenakshi Thirukalyanam (divine marriage) during the Chithirai Festival attracts thousands. Other festivals include Navratri, Avani Moolam, and float festivals at the temple's sacred pond.
