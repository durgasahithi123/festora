The Mahabharata is one of India's greatest epics, narrating the tale of the Kuru dynasty and the monumental Kurukshetra War between the Pandavas and Kauravas. Rich with stories of heroism, dharma, and spirituality, it also contains the philosophical Bhagavad Gita. The Mahabharata has inspired countless adaptations in literature, film, and television.
