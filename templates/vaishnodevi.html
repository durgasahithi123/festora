Vaishno Devi Temple

History:
Vaishno Devi Temple, located in Katra, Jammu and Kashmir, atop the Trikuta Mountains, is one of the holiest Shakti Peethas. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi, a manifestation of Mahakali, Mahalakshmi, and Mahasaraswati. According to legends, the temple cave has existed for nearly a million years and is deeply associated with mythological stories involving the Pandavas and Lord Krishna.
Puja Rituals:
Devotees perform daily aartis and rituals in the sanctum sanctorum, offering flowers, sweets, and chanting prayers. The temple follows a strict tradition of worship, and pilgrimages include visiting various sacred points along the trekking route.
Festivals Celebrated:
Navratri is the most prominent festival celebrated here with great enthusiasm. Millions of devotees visit during this time for special prayers, cultural events, and rituals honoring the goddess.
