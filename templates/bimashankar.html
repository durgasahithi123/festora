Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga

History:
Bhimashankar Temple located near Pune, Maharashtra, is surrounded by the Sahyadri hills and dense forests. It is a revered Jyotirlinga site with significant spiritual and ecological importance.
Puja Rituals:
Daily worship includes traditional abhishekam, offerings of flowers and fruits, and chanting of sacred hymns. The temple atmosphere is serene and connected closely with nature.
Festivals Celebrated:
Maha Shivaratri is celebrated with special prayers and cultural programs attracting numerous devotees.
