Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga

History:
Grishneshwar Temple near Aurangabad, Maharashtra, is the last of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India. The temple exhibits beautiful Hemadpanthi style architecture and holds high religious significance.
Puja Rituals:
Daily pujas and abhishekams are conducted with traditional rituals honoring Lord Shiva.
Festivals Celebrated:
Maha Shivaratri and Shravan Maas festivals draw large numbers of devotees.
