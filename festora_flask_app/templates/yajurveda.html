Yajurveda

The Yajurveda is one of the four Vedas, primarily focused on ritual formulas and sacrificial rites in Hinduism. It contains prose mantras and instructions a priest recites while performing various ceremonies, particularly yajnas (sacrificial fires).
It has two major branches:
Shukla (White) Yajurveda: Mainly prose mantras and formulas.
Krishna (Black) Yajurveda: Contains prose and poetic verses mixed together.
