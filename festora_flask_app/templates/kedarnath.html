Kedarnath Temple

History:
Kedarnath Temple, nestled in the Garhwal Himalayan range of Uttarakhand, is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is believed to have been originally built by the Pandava brothers and was later resurrected in its current form by Adi Shankaracharya in the 8th century. Constructed with large stone slabs without mortar, it stands resilient amidst the harsh Himalayan environment at an altitude of 3,583 meters.
