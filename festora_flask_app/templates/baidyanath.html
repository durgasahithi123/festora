Baidyanath Jyotirlinga

History:
Baidyanath Jyotirlinga, located in Deoghar, Jharkhand, is famous for being a healing shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is believed that Lord Shiva here acts as a divine physician who heals devotees spiritually and physically.
Puja Rituals:
Pujas include traditional abhishekams, milk offerings, and daily aartis. Devotees also offer fruits and flowers to seek grace.
Festivals Celebrated:
Maha Shivaratri and Shravan Maas are celebrated with devotion and masses of pilgrims throng to the temple.
