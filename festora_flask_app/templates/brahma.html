Brahma Purana

The Brahma Purana is one of the eighteen major Puranas of Hinduism, traditionally attributed to sage Veda Vyasa. It is an ancient scripture primarily centered on cosmology, mythology, sacred geography, and religious teachings.
It combines mythology with moral instruction, providing guidance on religious rituals, temple construction, and moral living. The text underscores the significance of devotion and moral conduct in achieving spiritual progress. It has played a notable role in shaping Hindu temple traditions, pilgrimage customs, and understanding of sacred geography.
