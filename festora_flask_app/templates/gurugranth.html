Guru Granth Sahib

The Guru Granth Sahib is the central religious scripture of Sikhism, considered the final and eternal Guru by Sikhs. It consists of hymns and teachings from the Sikh Gurus and various saints, emphasizing devotion to one God, equality, and righteous living.

This scripture guides Sikh spiritual and moral conduct and is recited daily in Gurdwaras worldwide.
