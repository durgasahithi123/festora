Tattvartha Sutra

The Tattvartha Sutra by Acharya Umasvati is the most comprehensive and authoritative treatise of Jain philosophy, presenting a systematic explanation of Jain metaphysics, ethics, and logic. It concisely outlines the nature of reality, the path to liberation, and ethical conduct for both monks and laypeople.

This seminal text is widely studied by Jain scholars and provides a foundation for understanding Jain doctrines.
