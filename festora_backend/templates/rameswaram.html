Rameswaram Jyotirlinga

History:
Rameswaram Temple in Tamil Nadu is believed to have been established by Lord Rama to worship Lord Shiva after the Lanka war. It is a sacred pilgrimage site and part of the Char Dham temples.
Puja Rituals:
Daily rituals include abhishekam, offering of bilva leaves, flowers, and water to the deity. The temple is renowned for its elaborate pujas along with the chanting of hymns.
Festivals Celebrated:
Maha Shivaratri and Ramanavami are the major festivals attracting large pilgrimages.
